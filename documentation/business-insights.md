# Business Insights

## Hiring Trends

Business Analyst and Data Analyst roles have the highest number of job openings.

---

## Salary Insights

Salary consistently increases with experience level.

Leadership roles offer the highest compensation.

---

## Skill Demand

Business Intelligence, AI/ML, Data Engineering, and Data Science are the most demanded skills.

---

## Hiring Locations

Major hiring cities include:

- Mumbai
- Bangalore
- Chennai
- Pune
- Noida

---

## Company Analysis

Small and Startup companies contribute a large share of hiring.

Large enterprises focus more on experienced professionals.

---

## Work Mode

Hybrid and Remote opportunities continue to grow alongside On-site roles.

---

## Overall Conclusion

The Indian technology job market shows increasing demand for analytics, AI, and cloud-related skills while offering better salaries for experienced professionals.
