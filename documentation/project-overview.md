# Project Overview

## Project Name

Career Intelligence Platform

---

## Problem Statement

The technology job market is highly dynamic, making it difficult for job seekers, recruiters, and organizations to understand hiring trends, salary patterns, and skill demand.

This project addresses that challenge by transforming raw job posting data into an interactive analytics dashboard.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze hiring trends across India
- Identify top hiring companies
- Discover high-demand cities
- Compare salaries across experience levels
- Analyze skill demand
- Understand work mode distribution
- Explore company size trends

---

## Dataset

Indian Technology Job Market 2026

Total Records: 23,201

Source: Naukri.com

- Job Seekers
- Recruiters
- HR Teams
- Business Analysts

---

## Expected Outcome

The dashboard enables users to quickly identify hiring patterns, salary insights, skill demand, and workforce trends for better decision-making.
