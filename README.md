# 📊 Career Intelligence Platform

> **Interactive Workforce Analytics Dashboard for the Indian Technology Job Market (2026)**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Career Intelligence Platform** is an interactive workforce analytics dashboard developed to analyze over **23,000 Indian technology job postings**. It transforms raw job-market data into meaningful insights, helping users understand hiring trends, salary patterns, company performance, and skill demand.

The dashboard is designed for:

- 👩‍💻 Job Seekers
- 🏢 Recruiters
- 📈 HR Teams
- 📊 Business Analysts

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze hiring trends across India.
- Identify top hiring companies and cities.
- Compare salary distributions.
- Explore experience-level demand.
- Discover the most in-demand technical skills.
- Support data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Indian Tech Job Market (2026)

**Records:** 23,201+

### Includes

- Job Title
- Company
- Salary
- Company Rating
- Experience
- Location
- Skill Domain
- Work Mode
- Company Size
- Posted Date
